Song Rater

What is Song Rater?

  • A place where you can compile and rate every song you come across
  • You can rate any song, just like this one
  • You can give reviews for songs
  • View and comment on other users reviews
  • You can see how others have rated songs
  • For any given song, you can listen to a snippet of the beginning for referennce
  • Those who purchase our monthly premium membership may listen to any songs fully

What is it going to look like?

As you can see you can select songs, give them a rating of your choosing, and see how it compares to other users opinions
