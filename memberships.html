Song Rater Memberships

Here you can look at the different features that come with the different memberships of Song Rater

  • Free Membership
  • Paid Membership
  • Premium Paid Membership

Free Membership

$0.00
For the free membership, you will have access to the app, however you will not have access to all of the features. Here you will still be able to rate and comment on songs, however you will be unable to listen to them in the app. You will be given 10 uses per day to listen to snippets of the beginning of songs, but after that you will have to listen on another platform.
Back to Top

Paid Membership

$0.99
For the paid membership, you will have all of the features of the free membership, except now you will be able to listen to a snippet of any song of your choosing whenever you want.
Back to Top

Premium Paid Membership

$9.99
For the premium paid membership, you will have all of the features of the paid membership, except now you will be able to listen to any song of your choosing whenever you want. You are able to rate your favorite songs, and even create albums with songs you rate for listening at your pleasure. At song rater, you will now be able to listen to your favorite songs at a similar price as music apps, with the ability to now rate and see how others view the songs you listen to.
